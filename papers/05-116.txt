QUESTION:

What is RINT(+infinity)?  RINT(-infinity)?  RINT(NaN)?

DISCUSSION:

RINT(X) when X is an infinity shall be X.

RINT(X) when X is a NaN shall be a NaN, and should be the NaN
argument.
